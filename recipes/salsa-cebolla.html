Salsa Cebolla

Description

Salsa Cebolla is a recipe that I grew up eating. The origins come from the Andes mountain range in South America. This recipe was passed down by my grandparennts from Ecuador.

Used primarily as a topping, salsa cebolla is a take on Ecuador's national dish, ceviche. This fish and shrimp-free dish pairs nicely on rice or a meat of your choosing.

Ingredients

  • 6 Lemons
  • 2 Snmall Red Onions
  • 8 Roma Tomatoes
  • One bunch of cilantro
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Dice the red onions and put them into a large bowl
  2. Dice the red onion and place them in the bowl
  3. Finely chop the cilantro and add to the bowl
  4. Halve the lemons and squeeze into the bowl
  5. Add in 2 tbsps of salt
  6. Mix thorouhgly
  7. Cover the bowl with aluminum foil and place in the fridge for 30 minutes to let the flavors marinate
  8. Enjoy!
